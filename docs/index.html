The Fab Four

In October 1962, a quartet out of Liverpool, England released their debut single 'Love Me Do', kicking off the output of arguably the most prolific and influential band of all time. The Beatles, comprised of John Lennon, Paul McCartney, George Harrison and Ringo Starr, dominated the 1960s, revolutionizing the genre of pop music, crossing global borders, and blazing new trails in audio recording.
